Rolf's Weather

Services for those of us that need to know the weather.

  • Detailed snowfall forecasts including start times, end times, and total amounts (daily email when snow is in the forecast).
  • Interpretation of 7 and 14 day forecasts which include the likihood and timing of large storms (daily email when snow is in the forecast).
  • Winter services provided whenever snow or freezing rain is in the forecast; not limited to any particular months.
  • Hourly snow accumulation updates during snow storms.
  • Freezing rain reports available on a seasonal, monthly or daily basis.
  • Available in most major cities across Canada. All forecasts and measurements are performed at the nearest major airport.
    • Ottawa, Toronto, Montréal, Calgary, Edmonton, Halifax, St. John's, Vancouver, Charlottetown.

Sample Reports
Snow forecast issued March 12th, 2018

The snow should start late this evening (8pm - 11pm) and will continue all day tomorrow and into tomorrow night.

Total snowfall accumulations by Thursday morning should be between 12cm and 21cm with a small chance of being as low as 2cm or as high as 25cm.

Expected accumulation per day:

  • Tuesday: 5-10cm.
  • Wednesday: 7-11cm.

On both days, the temperature will be close to (and sometimes a little above) freezing. This should reduce accumulations on paved surfaces.
